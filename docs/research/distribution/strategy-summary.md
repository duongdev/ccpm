## Risk Management

### Key Risks & Mitigation

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|-----------|
| Low adoption rate | Medium | High | A/B test messaging, adjust positioning |
| Critical bug found | Low | High | Hotfix process, clear communication |
| Marketplace rejection | Low | Medium | Appeal process, GitHub fallback |
| Negative feedback | Medium | Medium | Listen, address constructively |
| Team capacity | Low | High | Automate metrics, clear priorities |

### Contingency Plans

**If adoption < 200 users (Month 1):**
- Analyze onboarding flow
- Improve documentation
- Create more video content
- Reach out to communities
- Adjust messaging

**If critical bug found:**
- Acknowledge immediately
- Provide workaround
- Release hotfix (48 hours)
- Clear communication

**If marketplace rejects submission:**
- Address specific concerns
- Resubmit with explanations
- Distribute via GitHub
- Community can still install
